Clean generation, right where you live

Distributed solar reduces demand on polluting generation, cuts line losses, and can pair with storage for outages.

The problem is visible

When power is produced far away, the costs are hidden: air pollution, fuel price volatility, and grid stress. Solar moves part of generation to the edge, closer to load, with predictable output and no combustion.
