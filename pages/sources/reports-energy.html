Our World in Data Energy dataset

What we used: Annual electricity from fossil fuels and annual electricity from solar power, indexed to the first year shown.

  • Dataset: Our World in Data Energy dataset (owid-energy-data.csv).
  • Fields: fossil_electricity and solar_electricity (terawatt-hours).
  • How we used it: For each country, we plotted both series as an index where the first year shown equals 100.
  • Snapshot date in this repository: 2026-01-04.

Documentation: https://ourworldindata.org/energy
